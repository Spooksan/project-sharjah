Know More!

Health Facilities

The Baby-Friendly Hospital Initiative (BFHI) is an internationally accredited program aiming to create a health care environment that supports breastfeeding as the norm

Work Places

All workplaces in the Emirate of Sharjah are expected to obtain the ‘Mother-Friendly’ accreditation

Nurseries

‘Breastfeeding-Friendly Nurseries’ provide information and support to mothers on how to maintain their milk supply and to continue to breastfeed.

Public Places

A public place has a responsibility towards the community, and should contribute to “A Right Start for a Healthy Future” by becoming ‘Mother-Baby Friendly’.
